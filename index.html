Frequently Asked Questions

How realistic are these practice exams?+
Our questions match the exact format, difficulty, and topic weighting of the official WSET exams. Each question has four options with one correct answer. All content is based on publicly available WSET syllabus materials and learning outcomes.
What do I get when I purchase a bundle?+
Each bundle gives you instant access to 10 full-length practice exams for your chosen level. You can retake every exam as many times as you like, with no time limit on access. Every question includes a detailed explanation of the correct answer.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) through Stripe. Your payment information is handled securely by Stripe and never stored on our servers.
Can I access my tests on different devices?+
Sign in with your account to sync your purchases across devices. Your purchase history is linked to your account, so you can start on your laptop and continue on your phone.
What does Level 3 cover?+
Our Level 3 tests cover the 50-question multiple-choice theory portion of the exam. The real Level 3 also includes short-answer questions and a blind tasting, which cannot be replicated online.
Is this affiliated with WSET?+
No. WineExamPrep is an independent study resource. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Wine & Spirit Education Trust (WSET). All questions are based on publicly available syllabus materials.
